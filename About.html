About Us

Priced Out serves as a web platform connecting smart and savvy shoppers
with the products they enjoy, at competitive prices.

We're ensuring our members can easily view and compare some of the best
products on the market on two of the world's largest ecommerce websites -
Amazon and E-bay.

We understand the importance of our member's buying decisions, and we
provide a space to discover products they love, share them with a friend or
family and find value in the goods they invest in.

How to Use

1. Sign-up on our Priced out homepage by clicking on the get
started button. Or, if you'd like to search for products on
the fly, click on a product tile on the homepage.

2. Compare the price and details of products you care about
across Amazon and E-bay.

3. Comment, like, and share the products you're considering on
your personal social media platforms to connect with family
or friends.
